Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert known for its layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, creamy mascarpone cheese mixture, and dusting of cocoa powder. Translating to "pick me up" in Italian, Tiramisu is a rich and indulgent dessert that balances the flavors of espresso and sweet mascarpone. Often served chilled, it's a favorite at Italian restaurants and homes alike, offering a delightful combination of textures and flavors that make it perfect for rounding off a meal or celebrating special occasions.
