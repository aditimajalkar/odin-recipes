Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular Indian dish known for its tender, marinated chicken pieces in a creamy, spiced tomato sauce. Originating from the Indian subcontinent, this dish has gained popularity worldwide for its rich flavors and aromatic spices.

The chicken is first marinated in yogurt and spices, then grilled or roasted before being simmered in a luxurious sauce enriched with cream and tomatoes. Served with rice or naan bread, Chicken Tikka Masala is a delightful fusion of Indian and British culinary influences, perfect for a satisfying meal.
